📍 Our Journey

OUR JOURNEY: Our Road from Vision to Impact

From a single vehicle in 2000 to ₹899 Cr+ in work orders — every milestone tells our story.

1
2000 – 2008

The Humble Beginning

M/s RC Gupta established, executing first transport contracts. Secured initial trust with giants like NPCIL and GAIL, delivering ₹4.5+ Cr with a basic fleet.

3
2015 – 2019

Scaling the Fleet

Diversified operations by introducing Buses and Multi-Utility Vehicles. Expanded regional footprint heavily with GAIL Gas Ltd., executing high-volume contracts worth over ₹160 Cr.

5
2024 & Beyond

Driving Innovation

Continuing the momentum with ₹207+ Cr in recent ongoing contracts. Upgrading to premium fleets (Innova Crysta, Volvo) and specialized vehicles (Ambulances), towards sustainable logistics.

2009 – 2014

Birth of Vajreshwari

Prateek Gupta & Sneh Gupta officially establish Vajreshwari Enterprises. Brought prestigious clients like ISRO onboard. Upgraded fleet to Scorpios & Boleros, achieving a 10x growth with ₹49.5+ Cr in contract value.

4
2020 – 2023

Scale & Recognition

Massive deployment (e.g., 89+ vehicles for IOAGPL) and introduced HCVs & Emergency Vehicles. Surpassed ₹477+ Cr in work orders and won the prestigious Best Entrepreneur Award on GEM Portal (Dec 2023).
