Meet Our Director

Sneh Gupta — Visionary Leader

"A visionary with big dreams, determined to redefine transportation and logistics across India."

An alumnus of NMIMS, Mumbai, Ms. Sneh Gupta began her career with Avanse Financial Services, gaining deep operational expertise in finance. Armed with that discipline, he pivoted to entrepreneurship — building Vajreshwari Enterprises from the ground up.

Today, she leads 3 companies — Vajreshwari Enterprises, M/s RC Gupta, and Krady Solutions Pvt Ltd — with a core team of 25 at headquarters and over 1,200+ employees across PAN India.
